Alfredo

Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is a pasta dish made with fettuccine, butter, and Parmesan cheese. As the cheese is mixed with freshly cooked, warm fettuccine and ample butter, it melts and emulsifies to form a smooth, rich cheese sauce coating the pasta.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • parmesan
  • pasta
  1. Cook pasta
  2. Make sauce
  3. Stir pasta with sauce
